Fitch Rates Palm Bay, FL's $50MM GO Bonds 'AA'; Outlook Positive

Fitch Ratings - New York - 09 Aug 2023: Fitch Ratings has assigned a rating of 'AA' to the city of Palm Bay, FL's $50,000,000 general obligation (GO) bonds, series 2023.
Bond proceeds will be used to fund the cost of roadway improvements within the city. The bonds are scheduled for a competitive sale on or about August 22.
Fitch has also affirmed the following Palm Bay ratings at 'AA':
--Long-Term Issuer Default Rating (IDR);
--Outstanding GO bonds, series 2019 and series 2021;
--Outstanding special obligation refunding bonds (taxable), series 2013 and series 2019.
The Rating Outlook for the IDR and GO bond has been revised to Positive from Stable. The Outlook on the special obligation bonds is Stable.
