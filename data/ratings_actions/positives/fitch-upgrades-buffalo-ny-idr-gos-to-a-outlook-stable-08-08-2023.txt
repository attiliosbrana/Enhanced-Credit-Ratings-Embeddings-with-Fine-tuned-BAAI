Fitch Upgrades Buffalo, NY's IDR and GOs to 'A+'; Outlook Stable

Fitch Ratings - New York - 08 Aug 2023: Fitch Ratings has upgraded the following ratings on the city of Buffalo, NY to 'A+' from 'A':
--Issuer Default Rating (IDR);
--$46.6 million of outstanding unlimited tax general obligation (ULTGO) bonds;
--$93.6 million of outstanding limited tax general obligation (LTGO) bonds.
The Rating Outlook is Stable.
