Fitch Upgrades Two Classes of COMM 2016-DC2; Outlooks Remain Stable; Resolves UCO

Fitch Ratings - Chicago - 16 Aug 2023: Fitch Ratings has upgraded two classes and affirmed 10 classes of COMM Mortgage Trust, commercial mortgage pass-through certificates, series 2016-DC2 (COMM 2016-DC2). Additionally, Fitch has assigned Stable Outlooks to the upgraded classes and the Outlooks remain Stable for the affirmed classes. The criteria observation (UCO) has been resolved.
