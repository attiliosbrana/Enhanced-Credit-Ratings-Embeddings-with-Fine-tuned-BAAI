Fitch Upgrades One Class of CD 2017-CD5; Revises Three Outlooks to Negative; Resolves UCO

Fitch Ratings - Chicago - 30 Aug 2023: Fitch Ratings has upgraded one class and affirmed 13 classes of CD 2017-CD5 Mortgage Trust Series 2017-CD5. The Rating Outlooks for classes E, F and X-E have been revised to Negative from Stable. The criteria observation (UCO) has been resolved.
