Fitch Upgrades and Affirms 20 Freddie Mac 2016 Vintage Transactions

Fitch Ratings - Austin - 21 Aug 2023: Fitch Ratings has upgraded 33 classes and affirmed 160 classes from 20, 2016 vintage Freddie Mac transactions. The actions include 25 upgrades and 86 affirmations on classes from Freddie Mac mortgage pass-through certificates (FREMF) and eight upgrades and 74 affirmations on classes from Freddie Mac structured pass-through certificates (Freddie Mac SPC). The rating actions include both long-term ratings and unenhanced long-term ratings. All Rating Outlooks are Stable.
