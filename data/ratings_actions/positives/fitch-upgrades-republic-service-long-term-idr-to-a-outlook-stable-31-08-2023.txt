Fitch Upgrades Republic Service's Long-Term IDR to 'A-'; Outlook Stable

Fitch Ratings - New York - 31 Aug 2023: Fitch Ratings has upgraded Republic Services, Inc and Browning Ferris Industries, Inc's (collectively RSG) Long-Term Issuer Default Rating (IDR) and senior unsecured debt to 'A-'from 'BBB+'. Fitch has also affirmed RSG's Short-Term IDR and CP ratings at 'F1'. The Rating Outlook is Stable.
