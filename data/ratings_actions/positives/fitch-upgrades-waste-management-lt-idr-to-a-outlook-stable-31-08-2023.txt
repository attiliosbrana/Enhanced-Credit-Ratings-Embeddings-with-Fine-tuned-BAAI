Fitch Upgrades Waste Management's LT IDR to 'A-'; Outlook Stable

Fitch Ratings - New York - 31 Aug 2023: Fitch Ratings has upgraded Waste Management, Inc's and Waste Management of Canada Corporation's (collectively WM) Long-Term Issuer Default Ratings (IDRs) and senior unsecured debt to 'A-' from 'BBB+'. Fitch has also affirmed WM's Short-Term IDR and CP ratings at 'F1'. The Rating Outlook is Stable.
