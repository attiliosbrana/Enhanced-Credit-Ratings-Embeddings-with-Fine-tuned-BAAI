Fitch Upgrades American Muni Power, Inc. Combined Hydro Bonds to 'A-'; Outlook Stable

Fitch Ratings - New York - 11 Aug 2023: Fitch Ratings has upgraded the following American Municipal Power, Inc. (AMP) Combined Hydroelectric (Combined Hydro) project obligations from 'BBB+' to 'A-':
--Approximately $2 billion outstanding project revenue bonds.
The Rating Outlook is Stable.
