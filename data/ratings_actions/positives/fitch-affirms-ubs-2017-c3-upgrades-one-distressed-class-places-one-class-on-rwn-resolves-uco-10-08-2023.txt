Fitch Affirms UBS 2017-C3; Upgrades One Distressed Class and Places One Class on RWN; Resolves UCO

Fitch Ratings - Chicago - 10 Aug 2023: Fitch Ratings has affirmed 11 classes, and upgraded one distressed class, of UBS Commercial Mortgage Trust 2017-C3 commercial mortgage pass-through certificates. The Rating Outlooks for classes E-RR and F-RR remain Negative and Class A-S has been placed on Rating Watch Negative (RWN). The criteria observation (UCO) has been resolved.
