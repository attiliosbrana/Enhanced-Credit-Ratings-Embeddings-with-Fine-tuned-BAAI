Fitch Revises Philadelphia Airport's Outlook to Positive; Affirms GARBs at 'A'

Fitch Ratings - New York - 01 Sep 2023: Fitch Ratings has affirmed the 'A' rating on Philadelphia International Airport's (PHL) approximately $1.45 billion of general airport revenue and revenue refunding bonds (GARBs). The Rating Outlook has been revised to Positive from Stable. PHL has an additional $110.2 million of outstanding parity debt, which is a direct loan and is unrated by Fitch.
