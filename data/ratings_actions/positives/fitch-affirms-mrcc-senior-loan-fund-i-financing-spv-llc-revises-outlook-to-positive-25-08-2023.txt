Fitch Affirms MRCC Senior Loan Fund I Financing SPV, LLC; Revises Outlook to Positive

Fitch Ratings - Chicago - 25 Aug 2023: Fitch Ratings has affirmed the rating of the MRCC Senior Loan Fund I Financing SPV, LLC (MRCC) revolving loan facility. The Rating Outlook on the loan facility is revised to Positive from Stable.
