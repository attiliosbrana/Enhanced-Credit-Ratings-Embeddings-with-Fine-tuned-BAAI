Fitch Upgrades MSJP 2015-HAUL; Outlooks Remain Positive

Fitch Ratings - New York - 16 Aug 2023: Fitch Ratings has upgraded two classes and affirmed five classes of MSJP Commercial Mortgage Securities Trust 2015-HAUL (MSJP 2015-HAUL). In addition, the Rating Outlooks on classes D and E are Positive following the upgrade.
