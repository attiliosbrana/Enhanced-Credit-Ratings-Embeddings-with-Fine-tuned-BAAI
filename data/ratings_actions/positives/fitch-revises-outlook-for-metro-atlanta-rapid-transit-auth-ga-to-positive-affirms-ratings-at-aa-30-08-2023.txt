Fitch Revises Outlook for Metro Atlanta Rapid Transit Auth, GA to Positive; Affirms Ratings at 'AA'

Fitch Ratings - New York - 30 Aug 2023: Fitch Ratings has affirmed the Metropolitan Atlanta Rapid Transit Authority, GA's (MARTA) Issuer Default Rating (IDR) and its outstanding rated sales tax revenue bonds at 'AA'.
The Rating Outlook is Revised to Positive from Stable.
