Fitch Affirms and Assigns Positive Rating Outlooks to Freddie Mac's Commercial Servicer Ratings

Fitch Ratings - New York - 24 Aug 2023: Fitch Ratings has affirmed the commercial master servicer rating of Freddie Mac, Multifamily Division (Freddie Mac) at 'CMS1-' and the commercial special servicer rating at 'CSS2+'. Concurrently, both ratings have been assigned a Positive Rating Outlook reflecting additional demonstrated servicing performance and operational improvements yoy.
