Fitch Upgrades North Oaks Health System's (LA) IDR to 'A' from 'A-'; Outlook Stable

Fitch Ratings - Austin - 17 Aug 2023: Fitch Ratings has upgraded to 'A' from 'A-' the long-term rating on series 2021 refunding bonds issued by the Hospital Service District No. 1 of the Parish of Tangipahoa, LA (North Oaks Health System or North Oaks). Fitch has also upgraded the North Oaks Issuer Default Rating (IDR) to 'A'.
The Rating Outlook is Stable.
