Fitch Revises Nassau County, FL's Outlook to Positive; Affirms IDR at 'AA' & NAV Bonds at 'AA-'

Fitch Ratings - New York - 30 Aug 2023: Fitch Ratings has affirmed the Issuer Default Rating (IDR) of Nassau County, Florida, at 'AA' and the county's series 2007 public improvement revenue and refunding bonds at 'AA-'.
The Rating Outlook has been revised to Positive from Stable.
