Fitch Rates Pima County, AZ's Ser. 2023 Sewer Rev Obligations at 'AA'; Outlook Revised to Positive

Fitch Ratings - New York - 17 Aug 2023: Fitch Ratings has assigned a 'AA' rating to the following Pima County, AZ's (the county) sewer system (the system) revenue obligations:
--Approximately $61 million sewer system revenue and revenue refunding obligations series 2023.
The bonds are scheduled for sale via negotiation on Sept. 28. Bond proceeds will be used for capital improvement projects, to refund outstanding obligations for debt service savings, and to pay associated issuance costs.
Fitch has also affirmed the 'AA' rating on approximately $350 million in outstanding sewer system revenue obligations.
In addition, Fitch has assessed the utility's standalone credit profile (SCP) at 'aa'.
The Rating Outlook has been revised to Positive from Stable.
