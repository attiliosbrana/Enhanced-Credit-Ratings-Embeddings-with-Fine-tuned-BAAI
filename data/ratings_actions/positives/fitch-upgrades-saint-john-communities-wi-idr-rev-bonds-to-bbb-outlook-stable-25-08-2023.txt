Fitch Upgrades Saint John's Communities, WI's IDR and Rev Bonds to 'BBB'; Outlook Stable

Fitch Ratings - Chicago - 25 Aug 2023: Fitch Ratings has upgraded Saint John's Communities, WI's (SJC) Issuer Default Rating (IDR) and the bonds issued on behalf of SJC by the Wisconsin Health and Educational Facilities Authority (WHEFA) to 'BBB' from 'BBB-'. The Rating Outlook for SJC is Stable.
