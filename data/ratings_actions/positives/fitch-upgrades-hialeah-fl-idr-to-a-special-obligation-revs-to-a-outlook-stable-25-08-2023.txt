Fitch Upgrades Hialeah, FL's IDR to 'A+' and Special Obligation Revs to 'A'; Outlook Stable

Fitch Ratings - New York - 25 Aug 2023: Fitch Ratings has upgraded the following ratings for Hialeah, FL:
--Issuer Default Rating (IDR) to 'A+' from 'A';
--$20,190,000 outstanding special obligation taxable revenue bonds, series 2015A to 'A' from 'A-';
--$15,485,000 outstanding special obligation refunding revenue bonds, series 2015B to 'A' from 'A-'.
The Rating Outlook is Stable.
