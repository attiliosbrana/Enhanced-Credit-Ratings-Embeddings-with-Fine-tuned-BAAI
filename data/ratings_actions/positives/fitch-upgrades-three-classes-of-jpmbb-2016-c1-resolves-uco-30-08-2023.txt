Fitch Upgrades Three Classes of JPMBB 2016-C1; Resolves UCO

Fitch Ratings - New York - 30 Aug 2023: Fitch Ratings has upgraded three classes and affirmed 11 classes of JPMBB Commercial Mortgage Securities Trust 2016-C1 commercial mortgage pass-through certificates. Class F has received a Stable Rating Outlook, following the rating upgrade. The under criteria observation (UCO) has been resolved.
