Fitch Downgrades Four Classes of BANK 2017-BNK8; Four Negative Outlooks Assigned; Resolves UCO

Fitch Ratings - New York - 07 Aug 2023: Fitch Ratings has downgraded four and affirmed nine classes of BANK 2017-BNK8 Commercial Mortgage Pass-Through Certificates, Series 2017-BNK8. Fitch has assigned Negative Rating Outlooks on classes D, E, X-D and X-E following their downgrades. The under criteria observation (UCO) has been resolved.
