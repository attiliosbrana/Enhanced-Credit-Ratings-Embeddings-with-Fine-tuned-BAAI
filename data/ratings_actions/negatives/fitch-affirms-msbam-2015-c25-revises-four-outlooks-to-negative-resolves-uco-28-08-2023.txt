Fitch Affirms MSBAM 2015-C25; Revises Four Outlooks to Negative; Resolves UCO

Fitch Ratings - Chicago - 28 Aug 2023: Fitch Ratings has affirmed 13 classes of Morgan Stanley Bank of America Merrill Lynch Trust, commercial mortgage pass-through certificates, series 2015-C25 (MSBAM 2015-C25). Fitch has revised the Rating Outlook on classes D, X-D, E and F to Negative from Stable. All other classes Outlooks are Stable. The under criteria observation (UCO) has been resolved.
