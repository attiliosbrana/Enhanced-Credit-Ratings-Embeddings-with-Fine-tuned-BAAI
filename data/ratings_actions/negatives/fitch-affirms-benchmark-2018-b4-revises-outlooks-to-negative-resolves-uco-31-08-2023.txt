Fitch Affirms Benchmark 2018-B4; Revises Outlooks to Negative; Resolves UCO

Fitch Ratings - Chicago - 31 Aug 2023: Fitch Ratings has affirmed the ratings for Benchmark 2018-B4 Mortgage Trust. The Rating Outlooks for classes E-RR and F-RR have been revised to Negative from Stable. The Rating Outlook for class G-RR remains Negative. The criteria observation (UCO) has been resolved.
