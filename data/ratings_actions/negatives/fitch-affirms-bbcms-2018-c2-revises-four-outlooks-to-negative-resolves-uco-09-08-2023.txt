Fitch Affirms BBCMS 2018-C2; Revises Four Outlooks to Negative; Resolves UCO

Fitch Ratings - New York - 09 Aug 2023: Fitch Ratings has affirmed 17 classes of BBCMS Mortgage Trust 2018-C2, commercial mortgage pass-through certificates, series 2018-C2. In addition, the Rating Outlooks on classes F, X-F, G and X-G were revised to Negative from Stable. The criteria observation (UCO) has been resolved.
