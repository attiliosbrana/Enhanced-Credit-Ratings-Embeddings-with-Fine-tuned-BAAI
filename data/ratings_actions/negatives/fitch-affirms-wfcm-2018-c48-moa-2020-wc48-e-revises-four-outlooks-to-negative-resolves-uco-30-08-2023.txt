Fitch Affirms WFCM 2018-C48 and MOA 2020-WC48 E; Revises Four Outlooks to Negative; Resolves UCO

Fitch Ratings - New York - 30 Aug 2023: Fitch Ratings has affirmed 13 classes of Wells Fargo Commercial Mortgage (WFCM) Trust 2018-C48 commercial mortgage pass-through certificates. Class A-3 has paid in full. Fitch has also affirmed the MOA 2020-WC48 E Argentic horizontal risk retention pass through certificate (2018 C48 III Trust). In addition, the Rating Outlooks on classes E-RR, F-RR, G-RR and the MOA 2020-WC48 E Argentic horizontal risk retention pass through certificate have been revised to Negative from Stable. The criteria observation (UCO) has been resolved.
