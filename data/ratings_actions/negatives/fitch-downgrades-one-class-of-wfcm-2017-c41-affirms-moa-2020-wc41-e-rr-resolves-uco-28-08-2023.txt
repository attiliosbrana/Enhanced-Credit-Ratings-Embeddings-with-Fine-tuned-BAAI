Fitch Downgrades One Class of WFCM 2017-C41; Affirms MOA 2020-WC41 E-RR; Resolves UCO

Fitch Ratings - Toronto - 28 Aug 2023: Fitch Ratings has downgraded one class and affirmed 13 classes of Wells Fargo Commercial Mortgage Trust, Series 2017-C41. Fitch has also affirmed the rating for 2017 C41 III Trust pass through certificate (MOA 2020-WC41 Class E-RR). The Rating Outlooks for Class E-RR and the MOA 2020-WC41 E-RR certificate was revised to Negative from Stable. In addition, a Negative Outlook was assigned to Class F-RR. The under criteria observation (UCO) has been resolved.
