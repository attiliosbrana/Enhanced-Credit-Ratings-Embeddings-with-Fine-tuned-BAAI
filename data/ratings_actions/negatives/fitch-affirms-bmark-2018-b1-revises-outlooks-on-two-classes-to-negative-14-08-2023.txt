Fitch Affirms BMARK 2018-B1; Revises Outlooks on Two Classes to Negative

Fitch Ratings - New York - 14 Aug 2023: Fitch Ratings has affirmed 13 classes of Benchmark 2018-B1 Mortgage Trust commercial mortgage pass-through certificates. The Outlooks on classes D and X-D were revised to Negative from Stable. The criteria observation (UCO) has been resolved.
