Fitch Revises Mount Saint Mary College, NY's Outlook to Negative; Affirms Ratings at 'BBB+'

Fitch Ratings - New York - 31 Aug 2023: Fitch Ratings has affirmed Mount Saint Mary College, NY's (MSMC) 'BBB+' Issuer Default Rating (IDR) at 'BBB+'. Fitch has also affirmed at 'BBB+' approximately $45.1 million of outstanding par (FYE 22) revenue bonds issued by The Orange County Funding Corporation (OCFC) on behalf of MSMC (series 2012A, 2012B, 2022A and 2022B).
Fitch does not rate MSMC's million direct purchase bond issued by Wisconsin Public Finance Authority with approximately $2.2 million outstanding as of FYE 22.
The Rating Outlook is revised to Negative from Stable.
