Fitch Revises Outlook of Piedmont Muni Power Agency, SC to Negative; Affirms Ratings at 'A-'

Fitch Ratings - Austin - 09 Aug 2023: Fitch Ratings has affirmed Piedmont Municipal Power Agency, SC's (PMPA) Issuer Default Rating (IDR) and the rating on the following electric revenue bonds at 'A-':
--Approximately $553.6 million electric revenue bonds.
The Rating Outlook has been Revised to Negative from Stable.
