Fitch Affirms Good Shepherd, PA's IDR and Revs at 'A-'; Outlook Negative

Fitch Ratings - New York - 21 Aug 2023: Fitch Ratings has affirmed the Issuer Default Rating (IDR) and revenue bond rating on various debt series issued on behalf of Good Shepherd Rehabilitation Network and Controlled Entities, PA (Good Shepherd or GSRN) by the Lehigh County General Purpose Authority at 'A-'.
The Rating Outlook is Negative.
