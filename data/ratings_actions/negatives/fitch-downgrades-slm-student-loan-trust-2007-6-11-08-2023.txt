Fitch Downgrades SLM Student Loan Trust 2007-6

Fitch Ratings - New York - 11 Aug 2023: Fitch Ratings has downgraded the class A-5 and class B notes of SLM Student Loan Trust 2007-6, from 'AA+sf' to 'Asf' and 'Asf to 'BBBsf', respectively. Fitch has also assigned Negative Rating Outlooks to both classes.
