Fitch Downgrades WFCM 2018-C46 and MOA 2020-WC46 E; Outlooks Negative; Resolves UCO

Fitch Ratings - New York - 23 Aug 2023: Fitch Ratings has downgraded four and affirmed 10 classes of Wells Fargo Commercial Mortgage (WFCM) Trust 2018-C46 commercial mortgage pass-through certificates. Fitch has also downgraded the MOA 2020-WC46 E horizontal risk retention pass through certificate (2018 C46 III Trust). The Rating Outlooks on classes D, X-D, E-RR, F-RR and the MOA 2020-WC46 E Argentic horizontal risk retention pass through certificate are Negative following the downgrades. The criteria observation (UCO) has been resolved.
