Fitch Downgrades Mach 1 Cayman Limited; Outlook Stable

Fitch Ratings - Chicago - 25 Aug 2023: Fitch Ratings has downgraded Mach 1 Cayman Limited (Mach 1) series A and B notes to 'A-sf' and 'BBB-sf' from 'Asf' and 'BBBsf', respectively and has assigned a Stable Rating Outlook.
