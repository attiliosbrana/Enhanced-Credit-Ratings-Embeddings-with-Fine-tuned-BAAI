Fitch Downgrades Metropolitan College of New York's IDR and Revs to 'CC'

Fitch Ratings - New York - 15 Aug 2023: Fitch Ratings has downgraded the Metropolitan College of New York (MCNY)'s Issuer Default Rating (IDR) to 'CC' from 'BB-' and $62 million of revenue bonds, series 2014, issued by Build NYC Resource Corporation on behalf of MCNY.
